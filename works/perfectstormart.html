Perfect Storm

24/03/07

At the start of the month amongst working on other pieces while doing school I got a bit obsessed with storm chasers or rather the idea of such a profession/phenomena. I just think the idea of chasing down something so monolithic and unforgiving like Nature's finest equalizer is awe-inspiring first before it is foolish (Not that I believe in Social Darwinism). I can't for the life of me remember where I found/saw what I used as a reference for this, but I'm no longer too concerned with that notion. Something I wish I communicated a little better with this was the sheer size and form of the tornado, but I had other things I had to do. This is the last of my series depicting pieces of guys looking away from the camera with their arms raised (It's kinda cool that I get to call it a series because I want to)
