Personal Note

Whenever I'm looking back at old photos of myself, it feels like attending a funeral for each and everyone of my past selves and who I could've been. I know the best way to go about it is to look back with more sober and knowledgeable eyes, but sometimes I just cant. nostalgia is a poison and a provocative plight of man
